##### **# CMOS Inverter Design using LTspice**



###### *## Overview*

This project demonstrates the design and analysis of a CMOS inverter using LTspice.



###### *## Contents*

\- MOSFET Models

\- Circuit Design

\- DC Analysis

\- AC Analysis

\- Transient Analysis

\- Propagation Delay Calculation

\- Power Consumption Analysis



###### *## Tools Used*

\- LTspice



###### *## Results*

\- Voltage Transfer Characteristics (VTC)

\- Gain

\- Propagation Delay

\- Power Dissipation
